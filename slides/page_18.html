Key Stats & Targets

Ethiopia National Digital Payments Strategy 2026–2030

Digital Growth (2020–2025)

  • Mobile money accounts: 12.2m → 139.5m
  • Mobile banking accounts: 9.1m → 54m
  • Tx volume CAGR: 146%
  • Tx value CAGR: 161%

Market Size & Activity

  • Digital value: 82% of GDP (2024)
  • Target 2030: 750% of GDP
  • 30-day active MM: 15% (2025)
  • Target 2030: 60%

Merchant & E-commerce

  • POS terminals: 14,030 vs ~2m MSMEs
  • Merchant tx share: 0.16% (’23/24)
  • E-commerce share: <1% (2024)
  • Target 2030: 4%

Fraud & Security

  • Fraud losses (2024): ETB 1.3B
  • Fraud % of value: 0.0153% (underreported)
  • Target with reporting: 0.0008%
  • Cyberattack attempts (H1 2024): 4,623 (↑115% YoY)

Inclusion & Gaps

  • Rural–urban gap: 24pp → ≤8% (2030 target)
  • Gender gap: 10pp → ≤3% (2030 target)
  • Digital/financial literacy barrier: 66% women, 60% men
  • Regulatory: PSOs cannot operate escrow accounts (gap)
Source: Ethiopia National Digital Payments Strategy 2026–2030
