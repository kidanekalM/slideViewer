Policy Catalyst — Action 36

National Digital Payments Strategy 2026–2030

Exact wording from Page 51

Action 36: Review existing regulatory frameworks to permit licensed payment system operators to establish and operate escrow accounts at commercial banks and resolve the discrepancy between settlement timelines and e-commerce return policies. This review will assess the necessary risk management controls and legal frameworks required to enhance buyer-seller protection, reduce fraud, and build a trusted digital marketplace.

Source: Ethiopia National Digital Payments Strategy 2026–2030, Page 51
