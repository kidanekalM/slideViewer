Aligning Settlement & Returns

Fixing T+2 vs 7‑day buyer protection

Today, settlement completes in T+2 while many e‑commerce return policies allow 7 days. Escrow holds funds until acceptance or return window expiry, preventing premature release and protecting buyers without harming sellers.
