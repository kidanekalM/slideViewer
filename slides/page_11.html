Financial Projections

Illustrative projections based on adoption and fee assumptions

Year 3

500K transactions → USD 250M GMV

Year 5

2M transactions → USD 1B GMV

Assumptions

  • Average transaction size: USD 500 {World Bank SME Report}
  • Take-rate (fees): 1.5% blended {NBE Escrow Law}
  • Adoption ramp aligns with GTM phases and NBE Phase Two (2025) {National Bank of Ethiopia}
  • Numbers are illustrative for planning

Note: Projections are directional, not audited. Context: {IMARC Group} market growth; {National Bank of Ethiopia} Digital Payments Strategy.
