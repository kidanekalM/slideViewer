The Problem

  • Settlement occurs at T+2 while returns allow 7 days.
  • Funds can be released before the buyer’s return period expires.
  • Undermines consumer protection and trust in e‑commerce.

Policy Remedy (Action 36)

  • Permit licensed PSOs to operate escrow at commercial banks.
  • Align settlement rules with e‑commerce return policies.
  • Enhance buyer‑seller protection and reduce fraud.
